Mung Daal

This daal is made from husked and split mung beans. Best served with rice, and some red onion for garnish.

Equipment

  • Pressure cooker

Ingredients

  • Mung daal
  • Cooking oil for tempering spices
  • Mustard seeds
  • Cumin seeds
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Dry red chillies
  • Salt
  • Turmeric
  • Tomatoes
  • Lemon / lime juice
  • Garam masala
  • red onion (optional)

Steps

  1. Wash mung daal a few times until water is clear.
  2. Heat cooking oil in pressure cooker to medium high, add mustard seeds.
  3. Once the mustard seeds start popping, reduce heat to medium and add: cumin seeds, ginger, garlic, dry red chillies.
  4. Once the spices are fragrant, add mung daal and water in a 1:2 ratio to the pressure cooker.
  5. Add salt and turmeric
  6. Boil in pressure cooker for 1 whistle on medium.
  7. Release pressure, and add tomatoes / lime, garam masala, and reduce / add water until the desired consistency is met
  8. Serve, and optionally garnish with chopped red onion.
