Toor Daal (Pigeon Peas Daal)

This is a homemade Gujarati style daal - with a soup like consistency. Served best with basmati rice and full-fat yogurt!

Equipment

  • Pressure cooker
  • Immersion/hand blender

Ingredients

  • Toor Daal (dry daal works best)
  • Fenugreek seeds
  • Mustard seeds
  • Dry red chillies
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Asafoetida
  • Salt
  • Turmeric
  • Cumin + coriander powder
  • Daal masala
  • Red peanuts
  • Star anise / aniseed
  • Lemon/lime juice
  • Tomato (fresh or passata)
  • Jaggery / brown sugar

Steps

  1. Wash toor daal a few times until the water is clear.
  2. Boil daal and water (1:2.5 daal:water) in the pressure cooker for 2 whistles on medium heat.
  3. Blend with immersion blender until smooth.
  4. Add all other ingredients aside from lemon / lime juice, tomatoes and jaggery / sugar.
  5. Let boil, then reduce to a simmer, and adjust taste.
  6. Add lemon / lime juice, tomato, and jaggery/sugar for your desired acidity, colour & sweetness respectively. Ensure the jaggery melts fully.
