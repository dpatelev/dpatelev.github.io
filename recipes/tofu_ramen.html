Tofu Ramen

A quick and dirty tofu ramen broth, with pak choi.

Equipment

  • A smoothie maker / standalone blender, but any blender should do.

Ingredients

Broth

  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • White parts of spring onions
  • Sriracha
  • Soy sauce
  • Fish sauce
  • Oyster sauce
  • 250g block of tofu
  • 500ml vegetable broth
  • 1 small / medium sized pak choi

Toppings

  • Green parts of spring onions
  • Sesame seeds
  • Minced garlic
  • Squeeze of lemon/lime juice

Noodles

  • Ramen noodles work best, but soumen noodles are good for a noodle soup variation.

Preparation

  1. Press tofu block between 2 cloths / tea towels, for about 30 minutes.
  2. Chop the spring onions, and separate the white and green parts.
  3. Cut off the bottom of the pak choi, and wash the leaves.
  4. Prepare vegetable broth according to instructions.
  5. Cube tofu, and blend with half the vegetable broth, to ensure the resulting mixture does not clog the blender.
  6. Prepare noodles as per instructions.

Steps

  1. Fry garlic, ginger, and whites of spring onions in a pot until fragrant.
  2. Add the remaining vegetable broth to deglaze the pot.
  3. Add the blended tofu.
  4. Add sauces to taste.
  5. Add pak choi, and leave to simmer for about 10-15 mins. The leaves should reduce in size. Adjust thickness as desired.
  6. Assemble (noodles, broth, toppings), and serve.
